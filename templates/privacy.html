Privacy Policy
Effective Date: February 23, 2026

Gym Diary stores workout and account data needed for app functionality, including your profile, splits, and workout logs. Data is used only to operate the product experience and is not sold for advertising.
Data We Store
1. Account fields: username, email, optional profile photo URL, optional motivation note.
2. Workout data: split templates, exercises, sets, reps, weight, session dates.
3. Local preferences: theme and UI settings in your browser local storage.
Your Controls
You can edit profile data inside the app. For account/data concerns, use developer contact links in About.
